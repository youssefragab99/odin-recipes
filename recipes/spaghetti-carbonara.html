Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta or guanciale, cut into small cubes
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 100g Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • A small bunch of fresh parsley, chopped (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Cook the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water and then drain the pasta.
  2. Prepare the Sauce: While the pasta cooks, beat the eggs, egg yolk, and grated Parmesan together in a bowl. Season generously with freshly ground black pepper.
  3. Cook the Pancetta: In a large skillet over medium heat, saut√© the pancetta with the whole garlic cloves until the fat renders and the meat is crispy, about 5 minutes. Remove the garlic and discard.
  4. Combine: Add the drained spaghetti to the skillet with the pancetta. Toss well to coat the pasta in the pancetta fat.
  5. Remove the skillet from heat to ensure the eggs don't scramble./li>
  6. Finish the Sauce: Quickly pour the egg and cheese mixture over the pasta, stirring vigorously with tongs or a fork until the eggs thicken into a creamy sauce from the heat of the pasta. If the sauce is too thick, add a little reserved pasta water to loosen it.
  7. Serve: Serve immediately, garnished with more grated Parmesan, freshly ground black pepper, and chopped parsley if desired.
